Research Question: What is the historical impact of encryption and decryption technologies on military operations and strategic outcomes in warfare?

Why is my research question important?
Encryption and decryption have played critical roles in military history. By studying their impact, we can understand how technological advancements have shaped strategic decisions, battle outcomes, and the disruption of enemy plans. This includes examining how codebreaking efforts, such as the decryption of the Zimmermann Telegram or the breaking of the Enigma machine, have directly influenced the entry and success of nations in war. Exploring the historical impact of encryption and decryption technologies on military operations and strategic outcomes in warfare is crucial for understanding the past, informing the present, and preparing for the future in both military and civilian contexts

How I plan to continue my research:

How I plan to continue my research: I plan to continue my research by continuing to look at examples of how encryption and decryption have been used and continue to be used. I will delve deeper into historical case studies beyond World War I and World War II, exploring other conflicts such as the Cold War and more recent events like cyber warfare in the 21st century. This will include analyzing the role of encryption in key incidents and how breakthroughs in decryption impacted the outcomes of these events. I also intend to study the evolution of cryptographic techniques over time, from early ciphers to modern encryption algorithms. This will involve examining the technological advancements that have enabled more secure communication, as well as the developments in decryption methods.
